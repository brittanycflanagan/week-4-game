Crystals Collector


Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your number to the winning number, you will loose the game if your number goes above the winning number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
